1-How do I cancel my line ?

Contact the concerned team on the line 17882855 and they will assist you with the cancellation. Working hours: Sanday to Thursday (From 9AM to 5PM)

2- How to downgrade My mobile package?

Send an email to this address ecc@btc.com.bh and you will be contacted by the concerned team. Make sure to attach the required documents: Valid CR Copy,
 Authorized CPR Copy, Signed and stamped Application Form (Mobile-Postpaid-Application-Form.pdf (batelco.com))

please note that according to the terms and conditions, downgrading to a lower package before your contract ends may require a cancellation fee. This fee is calculated as the rental amount multiplied by the number of remaining months, in addition to any outstanding device or any nice number agreement penalties.

Downgrade fee: BD5.5
